# Staking FIS with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'
import { Steps } from 'nextra/components'

## How to Stake FIS with StaFi

### Required Wallets

**PolkadotJS wallet**:

- Install the PolkadotJS wallet extension for [Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd) or [Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/polkadot-js-extension/).
- Once installed, follow the prompts to create or import your wallet.

<Steps>

### Go to FIS staking page

Visit the [rToken App](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/all) and click the "Stake" button.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_1-new.png)

### Connect Wallet

Click on the “Connect Wallet” button to connect your PolkadotJS Extension wallet.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_2-new.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the amount of FIS you would like to stake. Review the amount of rFIS you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_4.png)

### Confirm transaction and await for confirmations

Upon clicking “Stake”, you will be redirected to your PolkadotJS Extension wallet. Review the transaction details, and click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_5.png)

### Track Rewards

Monitor your staked FIS and its rewards by visiting your [Profile](https://app.stafi.io/profile/polkadot).

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_6.png)

</Steps>

## The rFIS Token

StaFi Explorer: https://stafi.subscan.io/

Additionally, rFIS holders can participate in liquidity mining. To learn more about this opportunity, please [visit this page](https://app.stafi.io/profile/polkadot?net=StaFi).

## Commission Fee

10% of FIS staking Rewards and 0.2% redemption is allocated to StaFi DAO.

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Visit rFIS Dashboard

Visit the [FIS Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/polkadot/FIS?net=StaFi), and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" tab.

![StaFi Unstake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_7.png)

### Input unstaking amount

Enter the desired amount of rFIS you would like to unstake, and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Unstake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_8.png)

### Confirm transaction and wait for confirmations

After clicking "Unstake," you will be redirected to your PolkadotJS Extension wallet. 

Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. Click the "Sign the transaction" button to sign the transaction. 

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the unstaking process to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
Unbonding rFIS takes 14 days. FIS will be sent to your wallet after the unbonding period has ended.
</Callout>

![StaFi Unstake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_9.png)

### Withdraw unstaked tokens

rFIS will need to be manually withdrawn from the Withdraw Tab on the rFIS Dashboard once the unbonding period has ended. Click on “Withdraw” button to proceed.

![StaFi Unstake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_10.png)

### Receive tokens

Verify the transaction and the associated Gas fees on your Metamask Wallet. To proceed, click on the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. Upon confirmation of the transaction, FIS tokens will be withdrawn and sent to your wallet.

</Steps>
