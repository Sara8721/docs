# Staking SOL with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

## How to Stake SOL with StaFi

### Required Wallets

**Phantom**:

- Download Phantom from the [Phantom Website](https://phantom.app/).
- Once installed, follow the instructions to import an existing wallet or create a new one.

<Steps>

### Visit rSOL Dashboard

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/stakingunstakingothersol_1.png)

![Untitled](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/7bdf8039-88a9-4246-ba62-7e31400d7b65/Untitled.png)

### Connect Wallet

Click on the “Connect Wallet” button to connect your Phantom wallet.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/stakingunstakingothersol_2.png)

### **Input staking amount**

Enter the amount of SOL you would like to stake. Review the amount of rSOL you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

<Callout type="info">
Note: The minimum staking amount required is 0.005 SOL.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/stakingunstakingothersol_3.png)

### **Confirm transaction and await for confirmations**

Upon clicking “Stake”, you will be redirected to your Phantom wallet. Review the transaction details, including gas fees. To proceed, click on the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/stakingunstakingothersol_4.jpg)

### 6. **Track Rewards**

Monitor your staked SOL and its rewards by visiting your **[Profile](https://pre-app.stafi.io/profile/solana)**.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/stakingunstakingothersol_5.jpg)

</Steps>

## Commission Fee

10% of SOL staking rewards and 0.2% redemption fee will be allocated to StaFi DAO.

## Unstake

<Callout type="info">
rSOL mint and burn in StaFi Chain, if you want to unstake
</Callout>

<Steps>

### 1. **Visit rSOL Dashboard**

Visit the [rSOL Dashboard](https://pre-app.stafi.io/gallery/solana/SOL?tab=unstake), and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" tab.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/stakingunstakingothersol_6.png)

### 2. **Input unstaking amount**

Enter the desired amount of rSOL you would like to unstake, and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/stakingunstakingothersol_7.png)

### 3. **Confirm transaction and wait for confirmations**

After clicking "Unstake," you will be redirected to your Phantom wallet.

Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. Click the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. 

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the unstaking process to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
Note: Unbonding rSOL takes 5 days. SOL will be sent to your wallet after the unbonding period has ended.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/stakingunstakingothersol_8.png)

</Steps>
