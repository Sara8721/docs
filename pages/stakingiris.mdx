# Staking IRIS with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

## How to Stake IRIS with StaFi

### Required Wallet

**Keplr**

- Download Keplr from the [Keplr Website](https://www.keplr.app/).
- Once installed, follow the prompts to import or create a new wallet address

<Steps>

### Select Ecosystem

Visit the [rToken App](https://app.stafi.io/) and click on the “Ecosystem” button and select “Cosmos” from the dropdown menu.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_1.png)

### Connect Wallet

Click on the “Connect Wallet” button to connect your Keplr wallet.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_2.png)

### Visit rIRIS Dashboard

Click the "Stake" button to access the [rIRIS Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/cosmos/IRIS).

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_3.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the amount of IRIS you would like to stake. Review the amount of rIRIS you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

Note: The minimum staking amount required is XXX IRIS.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_4.png)

### Confirm transaction and await for confirmations

Upon clicking “Stake”, you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. To proceed, click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
Do not edit the memo shown on the Keplr transaction. Editing the memo will cause the transaction to fail.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_5.png)

### Track Rewards

Monitor your staked IRIS and its rewards by visiting your **[Profile](https://test-app.stafi.io/profile/evm?net=BEP20)**.

![StaFi Staked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_6.png)

</Steps>

## Commission Fee

10% of IRIS staking rewards will be allocated to StaFi DAO and validator.

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Visit rIRIS Dashboard

Visit the [rIRIS Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/cosmos/IRIS), and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" tab.

![StaFi Unstaked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_7.png)

### Input unstaking amount

Enter the desired amount of rIRIS you would like to unstake, and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Unstaked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_8.png)

### Confirm transaction and wait for confirmations

After clicking "Unstake," you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. Confirm the spending cap of IRIS and approve it.

Once confirmed, a second transaction pop-up for staking will appear. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. Click the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. 

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the unstaking process to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
Note: Unbonding rIRIS takes 21 days. IRIS will be sent to your wallet after the unbonding period has ended.
<Callout>

![StaFi Unstaked IRIS](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosiris_9.png)


</Steps>
