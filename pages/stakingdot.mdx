# Staking DOT with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

## How to Stake DOT with StaFi

### Required Wallets

**PolkadotJS wallet**:

- Install the PolkadotJS wallet extension for [Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd) or [Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/polkadot-js-extension/).
- Once installed, follow the prompts to create or import your wallet.

<Steps>

### Go to DOT staking page

Click the "Stake" button to access the [DOT staking page](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/all).

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_1.png)

### Connect Wallet

Click on the “Connect Wallet” button to connect your PolkadotJS Extension wallet.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_2.png)

### Select Polkadot Wallet

Switch to your Polkadot Wallet from the drop-down list shown.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_3.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the amount of DOT you would like to stake. Review the amount of rDOT you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

<Callout type="info">
Note: The minimum staking amount required is 0.005 DOT.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_4.png)

### Confirm transaction and await for confirmations

Upon clicking “Stake”, you will be redirected to your PolkadotJS Extension wallet. Review the transaction details, and click on the "Sign the transaction" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_5.png)

### Track Rewards

Monitor your staked DOT and its rewards by visiting your [Profile](https://app.stafi.io/profile/polkadot?net=StaFi).

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_6.png)

</Steps>

## The rDOT Token

StaFi Explorer: https://polkadot.subscan.io/

Additionally, rDOT holders can participate in liquidity mining. To learn more about this opportunity, please [visit this page](https://pre-app.stafi.io/pool/polkadot?net=Polkadot).

## Commission Fee

10% of DOT staking rewards and  0.2% redemption fee will be allocated to StaFi DAO.

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Go to DOT unstaking page

Visit the [DOT unstaking page](https://pre-app.stafi.io/gallery/polkadot/DOT?tab=unstake), and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" tab.

![StaFi Unstaked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_7.png)

### Input unstaking amount

Enter the desired amount of rDOT you would like to unstake, and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Unstaked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_8.png)

### Confirm transaction and wait for confirmations

After clicking "Unstake," you will be redirected to your PolkadotJS Extension wallet. 

Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. Click the "Sign the transaction" button to sign the transaction. 

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the unstaking process to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
Note: Unbonding rDOT takes 29 days. DOT will be sent to your wallet after the unbonding period has ended.
</Callout>

![StaFi Unstaked DOT](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotdot_9.png)

</Steps>
