# Staking SEI with StaFi

This document is intended for users and developers who want to learn more about StaFi Staked ETH and how to stake ETH using StaFi.

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

## Introduction

rSEI is the first LST based on EVM in the SEI eco. Developed by StaFi LSaaS Stack, rSEI addresses the challenge of balancing mainnet security and token liquidity within SEI.

## How to stake SEI with StaFi

### Required Wallet

**EVM Wallet**

- Download Metamask from the¬†[Metamask Website](https://metamask.io/).
- Once installed, follow the instructions to import an existing wallet or create a new one.

### Linking Sei and EVM / 0x Addresses

Make sure your SEI token is in the EVM address before staking SEI with StaFi. If not, please follow this guide to link Sei and EVM / 0x addresses.

- Navigate to¬†[https://app.sei.io](https://app.sei.io/) and connect to your Metamask wallet.

- Click ‚ÄúLink Addresses‚Äù after completing captcha.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/linksei.png)

- Sign a message from your Metamask wallet.

- Your addresses is linked as shown in the following picture.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/linksei_2.png)

- Transfer SEI token to the linked Sei address, then you can stake SEI on StaFi rSEI App.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/linksei_3.png)

<Steps>

### Go to staking page

Visit the [rToken App](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/all) and click the "Stake" button.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/stakesei_1.png)

### Connect wallet

Click on the ‚ÄúConnect Wallet‚Äù button to connect your Metamask wallet.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/stakesei_2.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the amount of SEI you would like to stake. Review the amount of rSEI you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/stakesei_3.png)

### Confirm transaction and await for confirmations

Upon clicking ‚ÄúStake‚Äù, you will be redirected to your Metamask wallet. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. To proceed, click on the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/stakesei_4.png)

### Add rSEI to your wallet

Navigate to the¬†[rSEI Dashboard](http://app.stafi.io/gallery/evm/SEI/?net=SEI) and click on the "Add rSEI to Wallet" button. A Metamask prompt will appear, select "Add token" to include rSEI in your wallet.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/stakesei_5.png)

### Track rewards

Monitor your staked SEI and its rewards by visiting your¬†[Profile](https://app.stafi.io/profile/evm?net=ERC20).

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/stakesei_6.png)

</Steps>

## Unstake

<Callout type="warning" emoji="üöß">
In the interest of security and stability, the unstake feature of rSEI is presently unavailable. It is scheduled to become accessible upon the full release of SEI V2 or around 2024 Q4. Meanwhile, StaFi will collaborate with protocols within the SEI ecosystem to enhance the utility of rSEI.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Go to SEI unstaking page

Visit the¬†[SEI unstaking page](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/evm/SEI/?net=SEI&tab=unstake), and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" tab.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/unstakesei_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

Enter the desired amount of rSEI you would like to unstake, and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/unstakesei_2.png)

### Confirm transaction and wait for confirmations

After clicking "Unstake," you will be redirected to your Metamask wallet. Confirm the spending cap of SEI and approve it.

Once confirmed, a second transaction pop-up for staking will appear. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. Click the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction.

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the unstaking process to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="warning" emoji="üöß">
Unbonding rSEI take 22 days before it can be withdrawn.
</Callout>

![StaFi Stake SEI](/image/unstakesei_3.png)

### Withdraw

rSEI will need to be manually withdrawn from the Withdraw Tab on the rSEI Dashboard once the unbonding period has ended. Click on ‚ÄúWithdraw‚Äù button to proceed.

### Receive tokens

Verify the transaction and the associated Gas fees on your Metamask Wallet. To proceed, click on the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. Upon confirmation of the transaction, SEI tokens will be withdrawn and sent to your wallet.

</Steps>
