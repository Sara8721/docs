# Staking SWTH with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

## How to Stake SWTH with StaFi

### Required Wallet

**Keplr**

- Download Keplr from the [Keplr Website](https://www.keplr.app/).
- Once installed, follow the prompts to import or create a new wallet address

<Steps>
### Go to SWTH staking page 

Click the "Stake" button to access the [SWTH staking page](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/cosmos/SWTH).

![StaFi Staked SWTH](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosswth_1.png)

### Connect Wallet

Click on the “Connect Wallet” button to connect your Keplr wallet.

![StaFi Staked SWTH](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosswth_2.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the amount of SWTH you would like to stake. Review the amount of rSWTH you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

<Callout type="info">
Note: The minimum staking amount required is 1000 SWTH.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked SWTH](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosswth_3.png)

### Confirm transaction and await for confirmations

Upon clicking “Stake”, you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. To proceed, click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
⚠️ Do not edit the Memo on the transaction. Your staking transaction will fail if you do so.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked SWTH](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosswth_4.png)

### Track Rewards

Monitor your staked SWTH and its rewards by visiting your [Profile](https://app.stafi.io/profile/cosmos).

![StaFi Staked SWTH](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosswth_5.png)


</Steps>

## The rSWTH Token

rSWTH: https://www.mintscan.io/stafi/assets

Additionally, rSWTH holders can participate in liquidity mining. To learn more about this opportunity, please [visit this page](https://app.stafi.io/rpool?program=lp).

## Commission Fee

10% of SWTH staking rewards and 0.2% redemption fee will be allocated to StaFi DAO.

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Go to SWTH unstaking page

Visit the [SWTH unstaking page](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/cosmos/SWTH), and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" tab.

![StaFi Unstaked SWTH](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosswth_6.png)

### Input unstaking amount

Enter the desired amount of rSWTH you would like to unstake, and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Unstaked SWTH](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosswth_7.png)

### Confirm transaction and wait for confirmations

After clicking "Unstake," you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. Click the "Approve" button to sign the transaction. 

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the unstaking process to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
Note: Unbonding rSWTH takes 31 days. SWTH will be sent to your wallet after the unbonding period has ended.
</Callout>

![StaFi Unstaked SWTH](/image/stakingunstakingcosmosswth_8.png)

</Steps>
