# How to Stake ETH with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

<Callout type="info">
Please be aware that the rToken app does not support Ledger devices. Therefore, it is crucial that you do not connect the app using a Ledger device. Connecting the app with Ledger may result in the loss of your assets.
</Callout>

## Dependency

**Metamask**:

- Download Metamask from the **[Metamask Website](https://metamask.io/download.htm)**.
- Once installed, follow the prompts to import or create a new wallet address

## How to Stake ETH with StaFi

<Steps>

### Connect Wallet

Visit the [rToken App](https://app.stafi.io/) and click on the “Connect Wallet” button to connect your Metamask wallet.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_1.png)

### Go to staking page

Go to the [rETH Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/evm/ETH?net=ERC20) and click on the "Stake" button to stake your ETH.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_2.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the amount of ETH you would like to stake. Review the amount of rETH you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_3.png)

### Confirm transaction and await for confirmations

Upon clicking “Stake”, you will be redirected to your Metamask wallet. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. To proceed, click on the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
Note: The minimum staking amount required is 0.005 ETH.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_4.png)

### Add rETH to your wallet

Navigate to the [rETH Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/evm/ETH?net=ERC20) and click on the "Add rETH to Wallet" button. A Metamask prompt will appear, select "Add token" to include rETH in your wallet.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_5.png)

### Track Rewards

Monitor your staked BNB and its rewards by visiting your **[Profile](https://test-app.stafi.io/profile/evm?net=BEP20)**.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_6.png)

</Steps>

## How to Unstake ETH with StaFi

<Steps>

### Visit rETH Dashboard

Visit the [rETH Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/evm/ETH?net=ERC20), and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" tab.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_7.png)

### Input Unstaking Amount

Enter the desired amount of rBNB you would like to unstake, and proceed by clicking on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_8.png)

### Confirm transaction and wait for confirmations

After clicking "Unstake," you will be redirected to your Metamask wallet. Confirm the spending cap of BNB and approve it.

Once confirmed, a second transaction pop-up for staking will appear. Confirm the transaction details, including gas fees. Click the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction. 

Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the unstaking process to be completed successfully.

<Callout type="info">
Note: Unbonding rBNB takes 16 days before it can be withdrawn.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/stakingunstakingevmeth_9.png)

</Steps>
