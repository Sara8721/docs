## How to Stake FIS with StaFi

### Required Wallets

**PolkadotJS wallet**:

- Install the PolkadotJS wallet extension for **[Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd)** or **[Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/polkadot-js-extension/)**.
- Once installed, follow the prompts to create or import your wallet.

<Steps>

### Select Ecosystem

Visit the [rToken App](https://app.stafi.io/) and click on the “Ecosystem” button and select “Polkadot” from the dropdown menu.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_1.png)

### Connect Wallet

Click on the “Connect Wallet” button to connect your PolkadotJS Extension wallet.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_2.png)

### Visit rFIS Dashboard

Click the "Stake" button to access the [FIS Dashboard](https://app.stafi.io/gallery/polkadot/FIS?net=StaFi).

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_3.png)

### **Input staking amount**

Enter the amount of FIS you would like to stake. Review the amount of rFIS you will receive and the Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for staking. Click the "Stake" button to proceed.

Note: The minimum staking amount required is XXX FIS.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_4.png)

### **Confirm transaction and await for confirmations**

Upon clicking “Stake”, you will be redirected to your PolkadotJS Extension wallet. Review the transaction details, and click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed and the staking operation to be completed successfully.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_5.png)

### **Track Rewards**

Monitor your staked FIS and its rewards by visiting your **[Profile](https://test-app.stafi.io/profile/evm?net=BEP20)**.

![StaFi Stake FIS](/image/stakingunstakingpolkadotfis_6.png)

</Steps>

## Commission Fee

10% of FIS staking rewards will be allocated to StaFi DAO and validator.

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Go to unstaking page

Go to the [FIS unstaking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/FIS?tokenStandard=Native) and click on "Connect Wallet".

![StaFi Staked FIS](/image/rfisunstake_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

To unstake FIS, you will need to provide the desired amount of FIS to be unstaked and then click on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Staked FIS](/image/rfisunstake_2.png)

### Confirm the transaction

Enter password to sign the transaction using your Polkadotjs wallet.

![StaFi Staked FIS](/image/rfisunstake_3.png)

### Waiting for completed

The unstaking operation will be successfully completed. Your tokens will be sent to your wallet.

![StaFi Staked FIS](/image/rfisunstake_4.png)

</Steps>
